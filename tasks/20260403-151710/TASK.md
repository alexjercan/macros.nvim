# When inserting a food item query for qty and insert macros too

- STATUS: IN_PROGRESS
- PRIORITY: 100
- TAGS: feature

I would like that after I query and select a food item, to go into another
query for the quantity. Once the quantity is also selected, insert the macros
too. This will help with not having to type in the quantity manually and then
hit the `<leader>mm` keybind. Instead it will be most likely `<leader>mt`. You
fuzzy find the food. Then you press enter, then you get prompted the quantity
and then you get everything.
